Youth Centers

Educational Facts

How Many Adolescents In The Uk Are In Poverty?

31% of adolescents throughout the UK are living in poverty as of 2026.

The Cost Of School

The cost of school necessities such as uniform, technology and trips create financial preasure on low income families and often results to adolescents in these situations to miss opportunities and become more isolated than their peers.

Mental Health Impact Of Poverty

Living with financial stress causes high levels of anxiety and worry for young people, impacting both their mental health and focus in class.

Housing Quality With Poverty

Living in cold, damp, or overcrowded housing is linked to higher rates of asthma and illness, causing students to miss more days of school.

Transport Barriers In Poverty

Limited or expensive public transport in local areas makes it difficult for adolescents in poverty to access youth services, job interviews, or after school clubs.

Rise In (Adolescent) Poverty

The amount of individuals, including adolescents, who are in poverty is still increasing despite small acts such as food banks and grants.
